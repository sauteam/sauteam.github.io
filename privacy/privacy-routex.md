# Privacy Policy for RouteX

**Last Updated: February 4, 2026**

This Privacy Policy explains how **RouteX** ("we," "our," or "the App") collects, uses, and protects your information. RouteX is committed to protecting your privacy while providing a seamless experience for recording your positions and light-chasing moments.

------

### 1. INFORMATION COLLECTION AND USE

To provide our core features, RouteX requires access to the following data:

#### **A. Location Data**

- **Purpose:** RouteX automatically records your position to calculate and capture "light-chasing moments" (such as sunset, sunrise, or specific astronomical events) based on your coordinates.
- **Access Level:** We may request **"Always Allow"** or **"While In Use"** location permissions. The "Always Allow" permission is necessary for the App to record your path or trigger alerts even when the App is running in the background.
- **Storage:** All precise location coordinates and history are stored **locally on your device**. We do not upload your real-time trajectory to our servers.

#### **B. Notifications**

- **Purpose:** We use local and push notifications to alert you of upcoming light-chasing moments, record status updates, or system reminders.
- **Control:** You can opt-out of notifications at any time through your device settings.

------

### 2. DATA STORAGE AND SECURITY

- **Local-First Privacy:** RouteX is designed with a "Local-First" philosophy. Your sensitive location history remains under your control on your iPhone, iPad, or Apple Watch.
- **No Third-Party Sharing:** We **do not sell, trade, or rent** your location data or personal information to third-party advertisers or data brokers.
- **Data Retention:** You have the right to delete your location history within the App. Once deleted, this data is permanently removed from your device.

------

### 3. THIRD-PARTY SERVICES

We may use limited third-party SDKs for the following purposes:

- **Map Services:** To display your recorded positions (e.g., Apple Maps).
- **Crash Reporting:** To collect anonymous technical logs (device model, iOS version) to fix bugs and improve stability. These logs do not contain your name or precise location coordinates.
